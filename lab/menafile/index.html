What's Menafile?

MenaFile is a leader in the field of total archive storage and management solutions, offering your institution all of the resources required to completely relieve you of all your archive related needs, from secure storage to quick retrieval, both in physical or digital form. Our state of the art facility, highly experienced team, unmatched logistics network and exceptional track record makes us your archiving partner of choice

How it Works

The MenaFile archiving team is deployed to your current archive storage area to catalogue and pack your files into our custom storage boxes with assigned barcode

Your boxes are transported to our state of the art facility for secure storage and the description of contents of every box are uploaded into our Records Management System (RMS) for easy retrieval later

Need a box, file, or document back? contact us and we will deliver it to you in record time, either in physical or digital form
