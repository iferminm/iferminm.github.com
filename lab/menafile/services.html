Our Promise

Security

24 – hour guarded security, intrusion detection, and a state of the art alarm system will make sure that your documents are always in the safest hands. In addition, your files will be placed in custom, standard size boxes, that do not bear the name of your company on them, this way, no unauthorized person in the storage facility will be able to identify the documents of a specific client. Not even our own employees can access your document without a proper authorization protocol.

Technology

We use state of the art software and bar-coding technology to accurately and effectively organize your company's records so that retrieval becomes an instantaneous process, from our warehouse to your desktop in less than the time it takes you to get it from your own archives.

Flexibility

The client only pays for exactly the amount of storage they require, versus overpaying for a facility they don't fully utilize, or over-utilizing a facility they are renting. Clients are billed per box/per month, and thus their storage requirements comfortably grow with us when they need it, and shrink when they don't.

Accessibility

Despite being safely and securely stored at our facility your records will always be at your fingertips. Whenever a particular record is needed, simply call, fax, or e-mail a request to us and we will deliver the box, or even the specific file to your location either within the hour, or if it is not urgent by the end or beginning of the next business day. Alternatively, if you do not require the original, a copy can be faxed to you, or a soft copy scanned and sent to your e-mail inbox.
